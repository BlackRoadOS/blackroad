## Core Mathematical Component: G(n)

**G(n) = n^(n+1) / (n+1)^n**

- Computed from pure integer arithmetic (exact rational representations)
- As n approaches infinity, G(n)/n converges to 1/e (~0.36787944117144...)
- The Amundson-Gregory Constant (A_G) = limit behavior
- kappa = A_G - 1 (the discretization gap)
- Computed to 10 million verified digits
- 50+ identities documented (536/536 identity tests in some repos)
- Not found in OEIS, ISC, or Wolfram at time of documentation
