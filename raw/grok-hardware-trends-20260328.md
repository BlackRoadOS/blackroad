## Key 2026 Hardware Trends

1. **Edge-First Inference Becomes Default** — 40+ TOPS on-device is the new baseline. Cloud is "insurance" not primary compute.
2. **Democratized Sovereign Stacks** — Pi 5 + Hailo accelerators (up to 40 TOPS for ~$100-150) enable real-time GenAI on cheap meshes. This is BlackRoad's exact hardware.
3. **Dedicated Personal AI Hardware** — Turnkey "AI PCs" shipping with high-end GPUs/NPUs + open-source OS layers.
4. **Hybrid & Distributed Architectures** — Composable inference across laptops, desktops, small clusters. One unified fabric.
5. **Efficiency Over Raw Power** — Inference favors low-power ASICs, quantized small models. Edge wins on TCO.
6. **AI in Everyday Devices** — Wearables, IoT, robotics embedding AI silicon. AI OS is ambient and personal.
