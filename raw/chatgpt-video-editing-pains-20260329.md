# ChatGPT — Video Editing Pain Points Deep Dive
**Date:** 2026-03-29
**Source:** ChatGPT (OpenAI)
**Context:** Why video editing destroys indie creators + how RoadView fixes it

---

## The Reality

- Simple 5-10 min video: **5-20+ hours** editing
- Shorts/Reels: ~30-60 min per finished minute
- Complex pieces: **30-40+ hours**
- Most creators spend more time editing than creating

## 7 Pain Points (Ranked)

### 1. Massive Time Sink
- Hours on trimming, syncing, captions, B-roll, transitions, reformatting
- Manual timeline scrubbing, layer management, endless revisions
- Workflow disorganization compounds everything

### 2. Rising Subscription Costs
- Adobe: ~$60-70/mo (with hikes)
- + Canva Pro + stock assets + AI credits (Runway, Pika, Midjourney) + music
- Pay monthly even during slow periods or lose access
- Constant financial pressure on inconsistent income

### 3. Inconsistent AI
- Cloud AI: short clips only (5-20s), poor continuity, weird artifacts
- No timeline control — generate then manually fix in another tool
- Token limits, internet dependency, training data risk
- "Soulless" outputs that audiences spot and tune out

### 4. Steep Learning Curves
- DaVinci/Premiere: complex multi-workspace interfaces
- Jumping between tools kills muscle memory
- Even "simple" tools struggle with pro features

### 5. Platform Optimization Hell
- Multiple versions (aspect ratios, lengths, captions)
- Motion graphics, color grading, sound design
- Solo creators can't master everything alone

### 6. Privacy & Dependency
- Uploading raw footage to cloud = data risk
- Tools change policies/prices overnight
- Limited offline = dead momentum during travel

### 7. Burnout
- Fewer uploads, lower quality, expensive outsourcing
- Creators question sustainability
- Audience skepticism toward AI-generated content

## RoadView Fix

- **Natural language editing**: "shorten intro, add zoom, match my brand colors"
- **Persistent memory**: remembers style, voice, brand DNA across sessions
- **Local & sovereign**: Pi fleet + Hailo, no subscriptions, no uploads, works offline
- **Integrated workflow**: one workspace with design, music, writing, agents
- **Speed**: fast local iteration, human oversight for authenticity

**Result: Hours saved per video. End monthly rent. Full ownership.**
