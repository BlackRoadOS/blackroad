## Small Language Models (SLM) 2026
- **Market**: $7.76B (2023) → $20.7B by 2030 (15.1% CAGR)
- **Enterprise spending on local models**: +40% YoY (Gartner)
- **Gartner projection**: By 2027, orgs use task-specific SLMs 3x more than LLMs
- **Cost savings**: 95% less than cloud LLMs for 80% of production use cases
- **Latency**: 50–200ms local response vs 500ms+ cloud
- **Quantization**: GGUF/EXL2 4-bit is industry standard for deployment
