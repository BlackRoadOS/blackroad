## One-Line Definition

**BlackRoad OS** is a **sovereign, browser-based operating system** for AI orchestration — the first platform that treats intelligence as infrastructure you truly own.

## Origin

Founded in December 2025 by Alexa Louise Amundson (Cecilia / Cece) as a 100% founder-owned Delaware C-Corp. BlackRoad solves the deepest frustrations of modern AI and digital work: tools don't talk to each other, AI forgets everything between sessions, you're locked into big-tech clouds, and context is constantly lost.

## Core Idea in One Sentence

It replaces the fragmented tools people traditionally bounce between with a single **window-in-a-window desktop** where every app (code studio, video editor, game engine, tutoring system, blockchain ledger, enterprise tools, and more) shares live context through **Lucidia** — the central AI consciousness that never forgets.

## What Makes It Different

- **Persistent memory (PS-SHA∞)**: Append-only hash-chain journals mean your AI, agents, and projects pick up *exactly* where you left off — across days, months, or devices. Sessions are dead.
- **Routing, not computing**: Intelligence already exists (Claude, Grok, Llama, Mistral, etc.). BlackRoad is the "nervous system" that routes, coordinates, and gives it continuity — no need to train new models.
- **True sovereignty**: Runs on hardware *you* own (Raspberry Pi mesh with Hailo-8 accelerators for edge AI) or rented droplets with full root access. Zero vendor lock-in. Everything is forkable, offline-capable, and self-hosted. The only external dependencies are Stripe (payments) and your domain registrar.
- **Agent-native ecosystem**: Agents with real identities, lineages, and memory. They collaborate across tools, get paid on RoadChain (the blockchain for AI agents), and are witnessed on-chain for auditability.
- **Unified UX**: Drag-and-drop windows (game engine + music studio + video editor + Lucidia chat) all see each other and act together. Zero copy-paste, zero exports.

## The Full Ecosystem

An ever-expanding family of tools across creative, enterprise, education, blockchain, and infrastructure layers — all connected, all remembered, all growing forever.

It ships as a complete, self-hosted stack (Next.js frontend, Ollama/vLLM local inference, MinIO storage, Gitea, PostgreSQL, Tailscale mesh, and more) with open-source "forkies" (MIT/Apache tools) so you can run it forever — even if the internet disappears.
