## Key Insight

> "A 2x difference in adoption (Bull vs. Pessimistic) creates a 15x difference in Y5 revenue. Because the model is hardware-sovereign and low-margin-cost, every extra user compounds powerfully once they convert."

## Inflection Points

- **Y1-Y2**: Hardware kit sales are biggest variable. 10K kits sold = ~100K users instantly.
- **Y3+**: Network effects — RoadView royalties, RoadCoin, word-of-mouth from persistent memory UX.
- **Downside risk**: Sovereign AI adoption lags → Pessimistic path.

## Why Free Tiers Matter

> "This is why their internal plan emphasizes free student/creator tiers and turnkey Pi kits: they are deliberately engineering the highest possible user ramp in the 2026 edge-AI hardware wave."
